**FAQ Content:**

### Getting Started
**Q: How do I create an account?**
A: Tap "Sign Up" on the welcome screen and enter your email and password. You'll receive a verification email to confirm your account.

**Q: Is DayGo free?**
A: Yes, DayGo is completely free to use with all features included.

### Habits
**Q: How do I add a new habit?**
A: On the Today tab, tap the green "+" button at the bottom right. Enter your habit name and tap "Create Habit".

**Q: How is my daily score calculated?**
A: Your daily score is the percentage of habits completed. If you complete 3 out of 4 habits, your score is 75%.

**Q: Can I reorder my habits?**
A: Yes, use the up/down arrows on each habit card to change the order.

### Mantras & Journal
**Q: What are mantras?**
A: Mantras are daily affirmations or motivational phrases that appear on your Today screen to keep you inspired.

**Q: How do journal prompts work?**
A: Create reflection questions that you answer each day. Your responses are saved and help you track your thoughts over time.

### Goals
**Q: How do I link habits to goals?**
A: When creating or editing a goal, toggle on the habits you want to connect. Completing linked habits contributes to your goal progress.

### Account & Data
**Q: How do I delete my account?**
A: Go to Profile tab → scroll down → tap "Delete Account". This permanently removes all your data.

**Q: Is my data private?**
A: Yes, your data is stored securely and never shared with third parties. See our Privacy Policy for details.

**Q: Can I export my data?**
A: Contact support@daygo.live to request a data export.

### Troubleshooting
**Q: The app isn't syncing my data**
A: Make sure you have an internet connection. Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, contact support.

**Q: I forgot my password**
A: On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password" to receive a reset link via email.
