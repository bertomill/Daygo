Demo account has pre-populated sample data to demonstrate app functionality.

To test core features:
1. TODAY TAB - View and complete daily habits. Tap checkboxes to mark complete and watch the score update.
2. DASHBOARD TAB - View 7-day progress history and streak.
3. GOALS TAB - View long-term goals and progress tracking.
4. PROFILE TAB - Access privacy policy and account deletion.

The app requires an internet connection to sync data.
